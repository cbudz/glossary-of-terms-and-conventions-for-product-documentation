[[z-series]]
==== zSeries (noun)
*Description*: "IBM Z" is the correct usage. Do not use "S/390x," "s390x," "IBM zSeries," or "zSeries."

*Use it*: no

*Incorrect forms*: S/390x, s390x, IBM zSeries, zSeries

*See also*: xref:ibm-z[IBM Z], xref:ibm-s-390[IBM S/390]
